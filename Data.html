Bar Graph

Data Table 3.3
     The bar graph shows the number of tsunami victims in several provinces in Indonesia. The data is divided into three categories: died, injured, and survived. Based on the graph, Aceh has the highest total number of tsunami victims, reaching almost 500,000 people. Sulawesi Tengah is the second highest with around 196,999 victims. Meanwhile, Bengkulu and Papua have the lowest numbers compared to other provinces. The graph shows a very large gap between Aceh and the other provinces. Most provinces have totals below 100,000, while Aceh is far higher than the others. If a tsunami occurs, it causes serious damage and many victims. It can be solved through sending much earlier warnings through the government. If the government improves early warning systems, the number of victims will decrease.

Line Graph

Data Table 3.4
      The line graph shows us the number of victims of the tsunami in different provinces of Indonesia. We can better understand the differences between the provinces by looking at this graph. The province of Aceh has the largest number of victims, which is 523,012 people. The Lampung Province also has a large number of victims, which is 108,215 people. The Central Sulawesi Province is another province with a large number of victims, which is 264,630 people. The South Sumatra Province has fewer victims than other provinces, which is 61,120 people. The Bengkulu Province and the Papua Province have fewer victims, which is 30,000 people each. The DKI Jakarta Province and the West Java Province have 120,277 victims and 88,896 victims respectively. The number of victims is not the same for all the provinces. If the tsunami is strong enough, the number of victims will increase too. This is why some provinces have a larger number of victims than others. If a strong tsunami occurs again, the number of victims will increase too.

Pie Chart

Data Table 3.5
     The pie chart shows the percentage of total tsunami victims by province. It explains how much each province contributes to the total number of victims. Aceh has the largest percentage, around 40.7% of the total victims. Central Sulawesi follows with 20.6%. Other provinces such as DKI Jakarta, Lampung, and West Java have smaller percentages. Papua has the smallest percentage among all provinces. Aceh has the highest the total percentage. If a province experiences a stronger tsunami, its percentage becomes higher. This explains why Aceh has the biggest part of the chart. If the tsunami is more severe in one area, its percentage will increase.
